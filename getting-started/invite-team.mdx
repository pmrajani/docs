---
title: "Invite your team"
description: "How to invite team members to your WapNotify workspace, assign roles, and manage access."
---

Every person who uses WapNotify in your workspace needs an account. Admins can invite members, assign roles, and deactivate accounts.

## Invite a team member

1. Go to **Settings → Team**.
2. Click **Invite member**.
3. Enter the person's **name** and **email address**.
4. Select a **role** (see [roles and permissions](/admin/roles-permissions) for what each role controls).
5. Click **Send invite**.

The invited person receives an email with a link to set their password and log in. The link is valid for 24 hours.

<Note>
  Invited users inherit the permissions of the role you assign. You can change their role at any time from **Settings → Team**.
</Note>

## Built-in roles

WapNotify includes two system roles that cannot be deleted:

| Role | What they can do |
|---|---|
| **Admin** | Full access — settings, billing, team management, all conversations and contacts |
| **Agent** | Access to conversations and contacts based on role visibility settings |

For most teams, Agents handle day-to-day customer conversations and Admins manage configuration.

## Custom roles

If you need finer control — for example, a role that can view analytics but not send broadcasts — create a custom role.

1. Go to **Settings → Roles**.
2. Click **New role**.
3. Name the role and toggle permissions on or off for each module (contacts, conversations, broadcasts, templates, leads, analytics, settings).
4. Set the **visibility scope**: what conversations and contacts this role can see.
   - **Own** — only records assigned to themselves
   - **Team** — records assigned to anyone in the same role group
   - **All** — all records in the workspace
5. Optionally enable **Share with peers** so agents with the same role can see each other's conversations.
6. Click **Save**.

See [roles and permissions](/admin/roles-permissions) for the full permission reference.

## Manage existing members

From **Settings → Team** you can:

- **Change a member's role** — click the member row and update their role
- **Deactivate a member** — removes their login access without deleting their conversation history
- **View last login** — useful for auditing inactive accounts

<Warning>
  Deactivating a member does not reassign their open conversations. Go to **Conversations** and reassign any open chats before deactivating someone who is leaving the team.
</Warning>
